What surprised me

Practical tips for getting started

  1. Start by understanding and extending existing work before proposing new ideas.
  2. Keep notes while reading papers, especially questions or unclear points.
  3. Write early, even when ideas feel incomplete.
  4. Talk through ideas with others; clarity often comes from conversation.
